About Blood Cell Classifier

Blood Cell Classifier is an innovative web application that classifies blood cell types using deep learning techniques. Powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (MobileNetV2), it aims to simplify the identification of blood cells for medical diagnostics and research.

Whether you're a researcher or healthcare professional, Blood Cell Classifier provides an intelligent solution to analyze blood cell images with precision. Our mission is to blend technology with medical science to enhance diagnostic accuracy.
